GOAL:

The goal of Training & Placement Cell is to provide world-class training and employment opportunities to PSCMR Students in Leading Organisations. A full pledged placement cell is functioning in our college with a full time placement officer.

AIM:

The aim of the placement cell is to provide the necessary information and skills to the students to compete in the market and find a placement. Those considering careers in education and related fields are offered detailed career guidance at the Training & Placement Cell. All counsel is based on advanced placement statistics and an envisioned view of future and educational and industrial trends. The goal is to help build a long term career in leading organisations and not just place the students.

Our Training and Placement Office performs the following activities:
  1. Training to meet diverse needs of industry.

  2. Invites various Industries for campus recruitment.

  3. Provides Effective Training in the areas of CRT, Technical, Interview Skills.

  4. Workshops and Seminars to enhance the student skills and learning capabilities

  5. Encouraging Students to attend Off-Campus Interviews

  6. Instant Training & Practice Sessions based on the Company requirement


The objective of the T&P Cell is to accomplish the following:
  1. Follows a policy of ‘One Person One job’.

  2. The Training & Placement service facilitate contacts between companies and graduates throughout the year.

  3. It also conducts parents meetings.
